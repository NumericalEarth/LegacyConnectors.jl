## Regenerating the W-K sounding

```bash
cd data/soundings
julia generate_weisman_klemp_1982.jl
```

The generator has no dependencies outside `Printf` (stdlib) and runs in
under a second. Tweaking parameters (e.g. qv_sfc cap, tropopause
height, shear depth) is a one-line edit at the top of the file.

## References

- Weisman, M. L., and J. B. Klemp, 1982: *The dependence of numerically
  simulated convective storms on vertical wind shear and buoyancy.*
  Mon. Wea. Rev., **110**, 504–520.
- Bolton, D., 1980: *The computation of equivalent potential
  temperature.* Mon. Wea. Rev., **108**, 1046–1053. (Saturation vapor
  pressure formula used in the generator.)
